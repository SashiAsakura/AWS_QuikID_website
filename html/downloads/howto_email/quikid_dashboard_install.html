Thank you for downloading
QuikIDTM Dashboard
You have completed downloading the QuikIDTM Dashboard on your computer. The next step is to download the free QuikIDTM app from the iTunes or Google Play App Store to your smartphone then, follow the steps below:
1. Access the QuikID™ menu by right- or double-clicking on the QuikID™ key icon in the System Tray or launch QuikID™ from the Start Menu or installation folder.
2. To pair your Phone with your computer:
- Ensure your computer and smartphone are connected to the same network.
- Select “Add QuikID™...” from the QuikID™ menu.
- Enter your username and password
- Scan the QR-Code with the QuikID™ app on your phone.
When the pairing process is completed, a “Pairing success!” message is presented and you can now unlock your computer using your smartphone.
